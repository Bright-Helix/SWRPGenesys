Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 9 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 150 XP
Starting Skills: Genosian begin with one rank in a skill of their choice. They still may not train this skill above rank 2 at Character Creation.
Industrious: A Geonoisan who provides assistance adds one Boost Die to the check in addition to the normal benefits of assistance, and the Geonosian heals 1 strain.
Winged: Geonosians can fly, but must land every five rounds.
