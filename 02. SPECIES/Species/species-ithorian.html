Starting Skills: Ithorians begin the game with one rank in Survival. They still may not train Survival about rank 2 during Character Creation.
Ithorian Bellow: With two mouths and four throats, Ithorians have a unique natural weapon they can call upon when threatened (Resilience; Damage 6; Critical 4; Range [Short]; Blast 3, Concussive 1, Slow-Firing 2, Stun Damage). Each time the Ithorian uses this ability, they suffer 3 strain.
