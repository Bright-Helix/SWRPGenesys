Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 100 XP
Sociable: Bimms begin the game with one rank in the talent Kill with Kindness
Two Species, One People: The Bimms are actually two species under the same name

  • Furred: Furred Bimms begin the game with one rank in Negotiation. Furred Bimms still may not train Negotiation above rank 2 at character creation. Furred Bimms are smaller than average and count as Silhouette 0
  • Near-Human Near-Human Bimms begin the game with one rank in either Knowledge (Education) or Knowledge (Lore). Near-Human Bimms still may not train Knowledge (Education) or Knowledge (Lore) above rank 2 at character creation. Near-Human Bimms are smaller than average and count as Silhouette 0
