Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Klatooinians begin the game with one rank in either Brawl, Ranged (Heavy), or Ranged (Light). In addition, A Klatooinian starts the game with one additional rank of one non-career skill of his choice. they may not train any of these skills above rank 2 during Character Creation.
