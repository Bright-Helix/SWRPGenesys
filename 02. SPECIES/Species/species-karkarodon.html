Karkarodon

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
3 2 1 2 2 2

Wound Threshold: 12 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 100 XP
Starting Skills: Karkarodons begin the game with one rank in Athletics. They still cannot train Athletics above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Amphibious: Karkarodons can breathe underwater without penalty and never suffer movement penalties from travelling through water.
Jaws: When a Karkarodon makes Brawl checks to deal damage to an opponent, they deal +1 damage and have a Critical Rating of 3.
