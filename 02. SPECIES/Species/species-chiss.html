Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Chiss begin the game with one rank in Cool. They still may not train Cool above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Infravision: Chiss have adapted to be able to see in both the infrared and normal visual spectra. This enables Chiss characters to remove up to 1 Setback DieStarWarsSetback added to checks by lighting conditions.
