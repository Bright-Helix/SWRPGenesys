Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Kaminoans begin the game with one rank in Medicine. they still cannot train Medicine above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Scientific Society: Kaminoans start the game with one rank of the Researcher talent.
Expressionless: Kaminoans add one Setback Die to all Charm checks they make. Other characters add one Setback Die to all social checks they make when targeting Kaminoans.
