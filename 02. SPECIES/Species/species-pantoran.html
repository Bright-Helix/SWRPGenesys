Wound Threshold: 10+ Brawn
Strain Threshold: 11 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 120 XP
Starting Skills: Pantorans begin the game with one rank in either Cool or Negotiation. They still may not train Cool or Negotiation above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Tundra Dwellers: When making skill checks, Pantorans may remove one Setback Die imposed due to cold environmental conditions.
