Barabel

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
3 2 2 2 2 1

Wound Threshold: 12 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 11 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 90 XP
Starting Skills: Barabels begin the game with one rank in either Perception or Survival. Barabels still may not train Perception or Survival above rank 2 at character creation.
Born Hunters: Barabels also start with one rank in the Expert Tracker talent.
Claws and Teeth: When a Barabel makes Brawl checks to deal damage to an opponent, they deal +1 damage and have a Critical Rating of 3. Additionally, the Barabel may spend four advantages to bite, inducing a critical injury as if it were a Vicious 2 rating weapon, and immobilizing the opponent for 1 round.
