Wound Threshold: 8 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 90 XP
Starting Skills: Sakiyans begin the game with one rank in either Perception or Vigilance. They still may not train Perception or Vigilance above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Superior Senses: Sakiyans's highly developed brains give them more adept senses than their human counterparts. Sakiyans may remove one Setback die imposed by lighting conditions, and add a Boost die to Perception checks involving olfactory or auditory senses.
