Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 9 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Gran begin the game with one rank in Charm or Negotiation. They still may not train Charm or Negotiation above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Enhanced Vision: When making ranged combat or Perception checks, Gran remove up to Two Setback Dice imposed due to environmental conditions or concealment (but not defense).
