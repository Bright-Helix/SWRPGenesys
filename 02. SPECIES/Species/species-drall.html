Drall

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
1 1 3 2 2 2

Wound Threshold: 8 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 12 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Drall begin the game with one rank in Knowledge (Education). They still may not train Knowledge (Education) above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Small Size: Drall are smaller than average and count as Silhouette 0.
Problem Solvers: Drall have a mind for problems and their solutions. In addition to using his skill or characteristic rating, a Drall adds one Boost Die to the dice pool when providing skilled assistance.
