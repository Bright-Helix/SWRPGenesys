Thisspiasian

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
2 3 2 2 2 1

Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 8 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Thisspiasians begins with one rank in Discipline. They still may not train Discipline above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Rest and Meditation: Thisspasians need very little sleep to function. They only need to sleep for two hours to heal all the strain they suffered from rather than a full night. (They cannot sleep multiple times in a day, however). In addition, once per session Thisspiasians may spend ten minutes meditating; if they do so, they heal strain equal to twice their ranks in Discipline.
