Besalisk

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
3 1 2 1 2 2

Wound Threshold: 12 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 7 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 95 XP
Starting Skills: Besalisks begin the game with one rank in Resilience. They still may not train Resilience above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Additional Limbs: Besalisks have multiple sets of arms. As a result, they gain an additional free maneuver per turn, though they still may not perform more than two maneuvers per turn.
