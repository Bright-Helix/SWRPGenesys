Clawdite

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
2 2 2 3 1 2

Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 9 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 105 XP
Starting Skills: Clawdites begin the game with one rank in Resilience. They still may not train Resilience above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Changeline:** As an action, a Clawdite may suffer 3 Strain and make an Average (StarWarsDifficultyStarWarsDifficulty) Resilience check. If the Clawdite succeeds, they change their appearance to match that of a Silhouette 1 character whom they have observed before. An observing character must make an opposed Perception vs. Deception check to detect that something is amiss with the impersonated character's likeness, mannerisms, or behavior. As always, the GM can add Boosts or Setbacks for situational effects that might affect the check, such as a Boost if the Clawdite's garb does not match the expectations or a Setback if the Clawdite has studied the impersonated individual's mannerisms closely.
