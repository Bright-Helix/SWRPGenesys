Xexto

1 3 2 2 1 2

Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 9 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 95 XP
Starting Skills: Xexto begin play with one rank in the Confidence talent.
Small Size: Xexto are smaller than average and are considered Silhouette 0
Additional Limbs: Xexto have six limbs: two legs and four arms. As a result, they gain an additional free maneuver per turn, though they still may not perform more than two maneuvers per turn.
