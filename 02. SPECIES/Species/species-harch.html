Harch

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
2 2 2 3 2 1

Wound Threshold: 11 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 11 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 85 XP
Starting Skills: Harch being the game with one rank in Perception. They still may not train Perception above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Additional Limbs: Harch have eight limbs: two legs and six arms. As a result, they gain an additional free maneuver per turn, although they still may not perform more than two maneuvers per turn.
Fangs: When a Harch makes Brawl checks to deal damage to an opponent, the attack gains the Stun 3 quality.
