Anomid

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
1 2 3 2 2 2

Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 105 XP
Starting Skills: Anomids begin the game with one rank in either Mechanics or Negotiation. Anomids still may not train Mechanics or Negotiation above rank 2 during character creation.
Claws: When an Anomid makes Brawl checks to deal damage to an opponent, they deal +1 damage and has a Critical Rating of 3.
No Vocal Chords: Anomids are born without vocal chords and are reliant on either their unique sign language when speaking to other members of their species or on a vocabulator mask, which may be purchased for 100 Credits.
Deep Pockets: Anomid characters begin the game with 1000 extra credits. (They may not spend these credits during Character Creation)
