Umbaran

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
2 2 2 2 2 2

Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Umbarans begin the game with one rank in either Cool or Deception. They still may not train Cool or Deception above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Entrancing Gaze: Umbarans add 1 Boost Die to Charm, Deception, and Negotiation checks
Shadow Dweller: When making skill checks, Umbarans remove up to two Setback Dice imposed by darkness. Umbarans add one Setback Die to all checks they make while in bright light, such as direct sunlight.
