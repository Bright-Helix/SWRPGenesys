Hardy Survivalist: A Whipid adds an automatic Success to Survival checks and can survive for several weeks without food thanks to reserve blubber.
Tusks: When a Whiphid makes a Brawl check to deal damage to an opponent, they deal +2 damage, has a Critical Rating of 4, and a Vicious 1 quality.
