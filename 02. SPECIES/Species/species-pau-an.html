1 2 2 2 3 2

Wound Threshold: 12 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 8 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 105 XP
Starting Skills: Pau'ans begin the game with one rank in Negotiation or Coercion. They still cannot train Negotiation or Coercion above Rank 2 during Character Creation.
Sensitive Hearing: Pau'ans add one Boost Die to all Perception and Vigilance checks they make as long as they are wearing protective earpieces. If they are not wearing these earpieces, they do not gain the bonus and they add Threat to Perception and Vigilance they make instead. A Pau'an begins the game with protective earpieces.
