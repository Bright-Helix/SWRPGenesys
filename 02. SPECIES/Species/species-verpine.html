Verpine

Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 12 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 90 XP
Starting Skills: Verpine begin the game with one rank in Mechanics. They still may not train Mechanics above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Hardened Carapace: Verpine begin the game with one rank of the Enduring talent.
Microvision: The compound eyes of a Verpine are capable of seeing tiny, even microscopic, details. When closely examining an object, Verpine characters add 1 Boost Die to their Perception checks.
Radio-wave Communication: Verpine can produce and hear radio waves, which allows them to silently communicate with other Verpine and specifically tune comlinks within a 100-kilometer range.
