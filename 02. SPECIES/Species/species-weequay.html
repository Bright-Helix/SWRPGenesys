Weequay

3 2 1 3 2 1

Wound Threshold: 10+ Brawn
Strain Threshold: 9 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 100 XP
Starting Skills: Weequay begin the game with one rank is Resilience or one rank in Athletics. They still may not train Resilience or Athletics above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Chemical Communication: A Weequay can communicate with other Weequay using pheromones. This is completely non-verbal, and undetectable to any other species nearby. Weequays can only communicate like this if they are within short range of each other.
