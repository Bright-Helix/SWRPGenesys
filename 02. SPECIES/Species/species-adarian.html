Adarian

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
1 2 3 1 3 2

Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 100 XP
Starting Skills: Adarians begin the game with one rank in Perception. Adarian still may not train Perception above rank 2 at character creation.
Long Call: Adarians may create a low sound which can be heard up to 20 kilometers away by other Adarians. Once per session, Adarians may also use it to attack using an easy Resilience check, dealing 4 strain damage to all non-Adarians within short range. This attack has a crit range of 4, but the user suffers 2 strain from using the attack
