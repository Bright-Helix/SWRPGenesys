Wound Threshold: 14 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 8 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 100 XP
Starting Skills: Wookiees begin the game with one rank in Brawl. They still may not train Brawl above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Wookiee Rage: When a Wookiee has suffered any wounds, they deal +1 damage to Brawl and Melee attacks. When a Wookiee is Critically Injured, they instead deal +2 damage to Brawl and Melee attacks.
Climbing Claws: When a Wookiee makes a roll to climb surfaces that could be pierced by their claws, they may add one Boost die to the roll. Claws can also be used for combat, though this is culturally forbidden, when a Wookiee makes a Brawl check to deal damage to an opponent, they may deal +1 damage and have a Critical Rating of 3.
