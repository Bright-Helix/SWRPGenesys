Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 11 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Twi'leks begin the game with one rank in either Charm or Deception. They still may not train Charm or Deception above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Desert Adaptation: When making skill checks, Twi'leks may remove One Setback Die imposed due to arid or hot environmental conditions.
