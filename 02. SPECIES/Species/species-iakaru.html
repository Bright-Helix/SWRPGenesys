Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Iakaru begin the game with one rank in either Piloting or Operating. They still may not train Piloting or Operating above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Brachiation: Iakaru suffer no terrain penalties through jungles or similar environments. They also add one Boost Die to Athletics and Coordination checks related to climbing, swinging, or balancing.
