3 2 1 2 2 2

Wound Threshold: 11 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 12 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 100 XP
Starting Skills: Aqualish begin the game with one rank in Brawl. Characters cannot train Brawl above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Amphibious: All Aqualish can breathe underwater without penalty.
Sub-Species Options: A player choosing to play an Aqualish character must choose between one of the following sub-species. No matter which sub-species is chosen, no skills may be trained above rank 2 during Character Creation.

  • Aquala: Aquala begin the game with one rank in Resilience. Furthermore, Aquala may remove one Setback DieStarWarsSetback imposed due to any cold or wet conditions
  • Ualaq: Ualaq begin the game with one rank in Survival or one rank in Perception. Furthermore, Ualaq may remove one Setback DieStarWarsSetback imposed due to dark conditions, but suffer one Setback Die to Perception checks in brightly lit environments.
  • Quara: Quara begin the game with one rank in Athletics or one rank in Coercion. Furthermore, Quara may remove one Setback DieStarWarsSetback when attempting to track anything through a natural environment.
