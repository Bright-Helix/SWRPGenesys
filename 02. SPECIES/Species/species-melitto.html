Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 11 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Melittos begin the game with one rank in Perception. They still may not train Perception above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Sightless Vision: Melittos can perceive their surroundings without needing light, and they never suffer penalties on combat or Perception checks due to darkness or other environmental factors that affect vision.
Reliant on Toxins: Melittos require the toxins of their native atmosphere to live. Melittos begin the game with free special respirators to emulate the conditions of their homeworld. They treat standard planetary air composition as a corrosive atmosphere with a rating 6.
