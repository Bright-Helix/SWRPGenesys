Drabatan

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
2 2 2 1 2 3

Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Drabatans begin the game with one rank in either Charm or Leadership. They still may not train either skill above rank 2 during Character Creation.
Big Noise: When making a Leadership or Coercion check. Drabatans can spend two Advantages to affect one additional character not originally targeted by the check or affect targets at one range band further than normal.
