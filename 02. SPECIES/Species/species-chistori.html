Chistori

3 2 2 2 1 2

Wound Threshold: 11 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 8 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Chistori begin the game with one rank in Brawl. Chistori still may not train Brawl above rank 2 at character creation.
Claws: When a Chistori makes Brawl checks to deal damage to an opponent, they deal +1 damage and have a Critical Rating of 3.
