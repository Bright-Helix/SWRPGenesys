Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn

Starting Experience: 110 XP
Starting Skills: Eloms begin the game with one rank in Resilience. They still may not train Resilience above rank 2 during Character Creation
Night Vision: When making skill checks, Elom remove one Setback dieStarWarsSetback due to darkness. However, they suffer one Setback dieStarWarsSetback in bright conditions.
Digging Claws: Before using a maneuver to move, an Elom may spend a maneuver to dig, allowing the Elom to use that move maneuver to travel through loose soil or similar terrain. At the GM's discretion, this excavation can result in a low, narrow tunnel that others can use.
