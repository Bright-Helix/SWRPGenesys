Ardennian

Brawn Agility Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence
1 3 1 2 2 2

Wound Threshold: 9 + Brawn
Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Experience: 95 XP
Starting Skills: Ardennians begin the game with one rank in either Athletics or Coordination. Ardennians still may not train Athletics or Coordination above rank 2 at character creation.
Small Size: Ardennians are smaller than average and count as Silhouette 0.
Brachiation: Ardennians suffer no terrain penalties through jungles or similar environments. They also add one Boost Die to Athletics and Coordination checks related to climbing, swinging, or balancing.
Additional Limbs: Ardennians have six limbs: two legs and four arms. As a result, they gain an additional free maneuver per turn, although they still may not perform more than two maneuvers per turn. While hanging, Ardennians may treat their feet as additional hands.
